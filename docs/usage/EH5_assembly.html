EH5_assembly¶

This script puts together the field slices that are computed by EH4_to_EH5.py.

EH5_assembly.h5_handler(filename, mode)[source]¶
EH5_assembly.vol_assembler(waveguide_id)[source]¶

This function will take the slices of the EH5 field and will assemble them together in a single array containing the fields for E and H and all their cartesian components.

It assumes that the slices have been generated by the EH4_to_EH5.py script.

Parameters¶

waveguide_idstr

The label for the job.

Returns¶

None
